Workflow

From citizen request to accountable infrastructure action

The workflow follows the domain expert guidance: public demand is captured, feasibility is reviewed, budgets are checked, officers assign work, engineers execute on-site, and QC validates closure.

01

Raise

Citizens or community representatives flag a public issue or improvement need with location and contact details.

02

Acknowledge

InfraLynx generates a reference number immediately, creating a visible starting point for tracking and follow-up.

03

Review & plan

Administrators and department officers evaluate feasibility, urgency, field requirements, and planning readiness.

04

Approve & execute

Budgets, quotations, and procurement checks happen before officers and engineers carry out the work on the ground.

05

Verify & close

QC certification and administrative validation are required before a project is marked complete for the public record.

Accountability by design

Why this portal strengthens trust and reduces corruption risk

The system is built around traceable approvals, controlled spending, and independent verification, so public work is harder to hide, delay without reason, or close without proof.

Traceable requests

Each issue gets a public reference number

Citizens do not need to rely on informal calls or personal influence. Every submission enters the system with a trackable identity.

Budget scrutiny

Funds move through multi-stage review

Field estimates, officer verification, quotation comparison, and finance approval create a stronger record before money is released.

Procurement checks

Vendor quotations and GST validation are required

Technical and financial justification reduce arbitrary purchases and make bill approval more disciplined and auditable.

Independent closure

QC certification comes before completion

Work cannot be casually marked done. A separate quality review helps ensure that closure reflects actual field performance.
